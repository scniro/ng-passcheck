  • Include the ngPasscheck module as a dependency in your AngularJS application 
angular.module('app', ['ngPasscheck'])
  • Tweak defaults and use results to craft the most elaborate UI, ever!

  • defaults: a mostly practical formula...

    strong passwords must be at least 12 characters in length, contain at least 1 lowercase letter, 1 capital letter, 1 special character, and 1 numeric character. The minimum score for a strong password is 65, the maximum, 100. Each additional character will yield a base increment of 1. Per character increment, numeric, capital, or special characters will yield a bonus increment of 1.5 per n occurance in password.
  • medium passwords must be at least 8 characters in length, contain at least 1 lowercase letter, 1 capital letter, and 1 numeric character. The minimum score for a medium password is 40, the maximum, 65. Each additional character will yield a base increment of 1. Per character increment, numeric, capital, or special characters will yield a bonus increment of 1.25 per n occurance in password.
  • weak passwords are any combination of characters, despite cryptologic strength, that do not meet strong or medium patterns. The maximum score for a weak password is the min value for a medium password. Each character will yield a base increment of 1.
  • isCommon - any password which, if optionally matched against 10k common passwords will automatically resolve to weak with a score of 0
